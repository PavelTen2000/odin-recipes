Chicken Bryan with Chicken Leg Quarters

Description

A copycat version of the famous chicken from Carrabba's Italian GrillÂ®, but made with leg quarters. Full of flavor, this recipe is company-worthy.

  • 2 tablespoons dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon prepared pesto
  1. Whisk white wine, oil, oregano, pesto, lemon zest and juice, garlic, salt, and pepper together for marinade in a small bowl.
  2. Put the chicken leg quarters in a resealable plastic bag and pour marinade over chicken. Seal the bag and shake to completely cover. Place in the refrigerator to marinate for at least 2 hours, turning over after 1 hour to ensure chicken quarters marinate evenly.
  3. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Bring chicken to room temperature while the oven heats.
